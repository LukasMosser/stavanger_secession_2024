About

Stavanger Secession is an international art summit happening annually in Stavanger combining discussion, projection, concert, performance and art installations.

It is modelled as an expedition bringing together artists, thinkers, musicians, filmmakers, architects, activists and scientists. It is short and reunites only a handful of participants from various geographies.

Stavanger Secession is an international art summit happening annually in Stavanger combining discussion, projection, concert, performance and art installations. It is modelled as an expedition bringing together artists, thinkers, musicians, filmmakers, architects, activists and scientists. It is short and reunites only a handful of participants from various geographies. Like in Stalker by Andrei Tarkovsky or the Mount Analogue by René Daumal, it is an expedition whose final goal is lost on the way. It’s “raison d’être” is its own existence which leads to various speculations, collision of ideas and the injection of fear and laughter in the exhausted routines of modern lives.
